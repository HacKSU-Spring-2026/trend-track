What it does

Every trend has a lifespan. We measure it.

From viral moments to slow cultural shifts — Trendlife gives you the data and the forecast to act before the curve turns.

📈

Lifecycle Forecasting

Prophet-powered time-series models trained on 5 years of weekly Google Trends data, forecasting 12 months ahead with confidence intervals.

☠️

Death Date Prediction

Every topic gets a predicted death date — the moment interest drops below the threshold of cultural relevance, with days remaining.

⚡

Trend Velocity

See how fast a trend is accelerating or decelerating week-over-week. Catch the inflection point before it's obvious.

🤖

AI Analyst Reports

On-demand AI reports written in plain prose — explaining what's happening, why, and what to expect next, based on real metrics.

⚖️

Side-by-Side Comparison

Compare two topics head-to-head: overlaid charts, matched metrics, and a comparative AI report covering which has the longer runway.

🔍

Phase Classification

Each trend is automatically classified into its current phase — Rising, Peaking, Declining, or Dead — with color-coded visual indicators.

How it works

From keyword to forecast in seconds.

1

Enter any topic

Type any keyword — a product, a person, a movement, a meme. Trendlife fetches 5 years of weekly Google Trends data instantly.

2

Model runs automatically

A Prophet time-series model fits to the historical data and projects 12 months into the future, including confidence bands.

3

Get the full picture

See phase classification, death date, momentum, volatility, velocity charts, and an optional AI analyst report — all in one view.

The story

Built in a 24 hours period, powered by real data.

Trendlife was conceived and built during Kent Hack Enough at Kent State University — one of the Midwest's largest collegiate hackathons.

The idea was simple: everyone talks about what's trending, but nobody tells you when it's over. Trendlife fills that gap with machine learning, real-time data, and AI-powered analysis.

Developed entirely by Dovlet Gurbanov, from data pipeline to UI, in a single hackathon sprint.
